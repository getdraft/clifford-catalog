# TimePay Team - DRAFT Onboarding

Welcome to DRAFT. Your product is now represented as a `software_deployment_pattern`
in this catalog, built from your `stack.yaml` and repo structure - read
`sdp.yaml` in this directory and tell us if anything is wrong. Five
RequirementGroups are active workspace-wide; your SDP includes draft
answers for all of them, but we know TimePay's nightly Spring Batch payroll
export doesn't fit the same mold as the other teams' pure-API services, so
expect to push back on at least one field if it doesn't model your batch
job correctly - that's expected and welcome.
