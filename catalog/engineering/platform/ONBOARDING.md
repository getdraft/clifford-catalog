# Platform Team - DRAFT Onboarding

Welcome to DRAFT. Most of what your team owns - the shared Terraform ECS
module and the platform conventions every other team builds on - has no
independent runtime, so don't be surprised if the SDP concept feels like a
poor fit. Where you do have a real deployable, the lightweight internal
developer portal, that part of sdp.yaml should look normal. Push back if
the rest doesn't make sense; that tension is expected and we want it
recorded rather than forced into a shape that doesn't fit.
