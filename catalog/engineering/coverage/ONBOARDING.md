# Coverage Team - DRAFT Onboarding

Welcome to DRAFT. Your product is now represented as a `software_deployment_pattern`
in this catalog, built from your `stack.yaml` and repo structure - read
`sdp.yaml` in this directory and tell us if anything is wrong. Five
RequirementGroups are active workspace-wide (security, data residency, SLA,
change management, vulnerability management); your SDP already declares
draft answers for all of them based on what we could infer from your repo,
but you own correcting anything that's wrong. You don't need to learn the
full DRAFT schema - just review the YAML, push corrections as a normal PR,
and ask platform-engineering if a field doesn't make sense for how Coverage
actually runs.
